The Company

Founded in 2015 in Ho Chi Minh City, Brownfield Capital Management is a specialist alternative investment firm focused on brownfield redevelopment, infrastructure, and real assets across Southeast Asia and global markets.

We bring institutional-grade discipline, rigorous risk management, and full transparency to private market opportunities.

Our Edge

  • Rigorous risk management
  • Full transparency & quarterly reporting
  • Deep local expertise in Vietnam & SEA
  • Proven track record in brownfield projects
